How to help

Truckerswelcome.ca is an open source project developed by volunteers in the tech community to help our front line workers who are facing significant challenges while on the road keeping goods and supplies flowing to our grocery stores, hospitals and communities. Truckers Welcome helps drivers find services that are still available near them during this crisis, using the location on their smartphone. This project is made possible by our generous sponsors and volunteers.

Sponsors

This project is hosted in the Google Cloud Platform, relies on the Google Maps API and uses DNS and Email services provided by EasyDNS. The cost for these resources are currently covered by our volunteers, we are currently looking for corporate sponsors to help with resources or funding to keep this project alive during the pandemic.

Thank you to Slack for providing a Standard plan free of charge for 90 days to allow our team to collaborate on this application.
